John Mitchell

Performed petrographic analyses of new and historic construction materials. Specialized in the evaluation of failed, deteriorated, and historic concrete, mortar, stucco, brick, dimension stone, terracotta, tile, etc. Available for contract geological exploration, troubleshooting, research and expert witness services for the aggregates, industrial sand, asphaltic concrete, and Portland cement concrete industries. Exploration geology specialties included aggregates and frac sand. Provided technical consulting for clients in the construction materials, historic renovation, industrial minerals, and related businesses. Committee work included participation in ASTM Cement (C1), Aggregate (C9), Road (D4), and Soil and Rock (D18) committees and subcommittees and in TRB committees "Concrete Durability" (AFN30), "Mineral Aggregates" (AFP70), and "Exploration and Classification of Earth Materials"
